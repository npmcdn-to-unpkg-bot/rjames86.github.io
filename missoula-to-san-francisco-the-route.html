Missoula to San Francisco: The Route

My plan is to depart on September 7 from Missoula, MT. I’ll be following the TransAmerica route all the way to the Oregon coast where I’ll meet up with the Pacific Coast Trail and ride south all the way to San Francisco.

My hope is that I can average roughly 100 miles a day for the entire trip, putting me at around 14 total days if I don’t stop.
